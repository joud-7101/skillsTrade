Our Story

Skills Trade was founded in 2025 by a group of university students who recognized the need for a more interactive and community-driven approach to learning. What started as a class project has grown into a platform with thousands of users across the region.

We're proud to be part of the University of Jeddah's College of Computer Science and Engineering, where innovation meets education.
